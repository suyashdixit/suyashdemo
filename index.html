Google I/O Contest

Win an all expenses paid trip to Google I/O for May 18th - 20th 2016!

Other exciting prizes to be won:

Upload a video on social media that tells us why you’re learning what you’re learning on Udacity. Best entries stand a chance to win amazing prizes.

What is this for?

Why do you learn? Why do you do the things that you do? Is it for recognition? Is it to get that job? Is it to climb the ranks in your organization? Or is it just because you’re interested in it?


Whatever the reason may be, it is this motivation that helps you cross the line; it gets the job done. We want to hear from you. Tell us why you learn what you learn on Udacity, so it can inspire others who relate with your motivations to learn.

How do I win?

It’s simple. Upload a video with the Hashtag #Udacity4IO on Facebook and Twitter.
This video must answer 3 questions:

  • Why this Nanodegree?
  • What do you expect to achieve from this Nanodegree?
  • What do you love about Udacity?

Am I Eligible?

This contest is open for all Udacity Nanodegree students in India, who are not the part of any existing scholarship program. If you wish to participate, and you’re not a Udacity Nanodegree student, head over to http://bit.ly/1WgY7ug, and enroll in a Nanodegree of your liking. Udacity Nanodegree graduates are also eligible.

How long does the contest run?

The contest runs from 14th March to April 10th.
